How Innovix HR helps businesses

Nowadays, managing employees the right way with the right tool can help your business run smoothly and more efficiently. Innovix HRM can facilitate to increase automation, reduce human error and ensuring you have a clear overview of your workforce. It can streamline your recruitment process, handle payroll efficiently and monitor staff development. Business services, like SOPs, job postings, timesheets, performance tracking and benefits can all be managed from one place, which makes overall management and decision-making easier. The use of Innovix HRM eliminates lag between data log and processing, and maintains data integrity at every step of the process. A summary of the main features:

  • Recruitment and placement
  • Employee data management
  • Attendance monitoring and time tracking
  • Payroll processing and compensation management
  • Training and employee development
  • Performance management, KPI tracking and evaluation
  • Analytics and reporting

INNOVIX HR FEATURES

  • Employee Module

    Easily store and access both personal and professional information of each employee within the organization.

  • Attendance Module

    Manage employees check in/check out procedure and monitor attendance reports.

  • Leave Module

  • Expense Module

  • Loan Module

  • Survey Module
