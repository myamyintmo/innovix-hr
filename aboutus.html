WHO WE ARE

Innovix HRM is a comprehensive HR Solution to fulfill the needs of today’s businesses that are moving towards a transformation from traditional to digital HR. Suitable for any kind of businesses, no matter the size, Innovix HRM facilitates numerous HR functions starting from recruiting employee, storing employee data, managing attendance and payroll, handling administration and record keeping up until employee exit procedure.

VISION

  • As per our motto – “Your Strategic HR Partner”, our vision is to be the ultimate HR Solution for Myanmar’s businesses by providing the essential tool to maintain your organization’s most valuable asset, human resource.

MISSION

  • Innovix HRM has dedicated our mission to helping the businesses with in-depth knowledge in consulting, customizing and deploying our HR Solution in Myanmar’s various business sectors today.
